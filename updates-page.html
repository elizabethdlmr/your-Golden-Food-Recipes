Today's recipe:

(Italian)

Lasagna

Your new favorite homemade dish!




ESTIMATED TIME (minutes):

  • Prep: 15
  • Cook: 85
  • Total: 100
  • Servings: 8

APPROX NUTRITION FACTS (per serving):

  • 815 Calories
  • 50g Fat
  • 32g Carbs
  • 61g Protein



INGREDIENTS

  • 2 tsps extra virgin olive oil
  • ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (3/4 cup aprox)
  • 1/2 large bell pepper, diced (3/4 cup aprox)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • (1) 28 oz can of tomato sauce
  • 3 oz tomato paste
  • (1) 14 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsps chopped fresh oregano / 2tsps dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, packed
  • 1 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder / garlic salt
  • 1 tbsp red / white, wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp / 1/4cup, sugar (optional)
  • Salt
  • 1/2 lb dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • (1) 1/2 lbs (24 ounces) mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  • 1/4 lb freshly grated Parmesan cheese



STEPS

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil, adding 1 tbsp of salt for every 2 quarts of water.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat 2 tsps of olive oil. Brown the ground beef, then transfer it to a bowl using a slotted spoon, leaving 1 tbsp of fat in the skillet.
  3. Cook diced bell pepper, onions, and minced garlic in the skillet until softened. Return the cooked beef to the skillet, reduce heat, and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Transfer the beef mixture to a medium-sized pot. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce and paste, parsley, oregano, Italian seasonings, garlic powder / salt, red / white wine vinegar, and sugar to taste. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15 to 45 minutes, stirring often.
  5. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water until al dente, then drain and rinse with cool water. Spread cooked noodles on an oiled baking sheet to prevent sticking.
  6. Preheat oven to 375°F. In a 9x13-inch casserole / lasagna dish, layer noodles, sauce, mozzarella, ricotta and parmesan cheese, repeating until all ingredients are used.
  7. Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil and bake at 375°F for 45 minutes, uncovering in the last 10 minutes for a crusty top if desired.
  8. Allow the lasagna to cool for at least 15 minutes before serving.
